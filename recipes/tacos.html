Tacos

Description

Tacos is one of the best dishes around the world. Tacos are very famous Mexican dish.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Meat (beef, chicken, fish, and pork).
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Chili powder or salsa

Steps

  1. Break apart lean ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat
  2. warm the tortillas
  3. Add chili powder, cumin, salt, oregano, garlic powder, and pepper
  4. Cook until the beef is browned
  5. EAT!
