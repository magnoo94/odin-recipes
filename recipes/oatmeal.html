Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a breakfast food made from oats and liquid like water or milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • Stawberries
  • 1 tablespoon of raw honey
  • Blueberries
  • Banana

Steps

  • Put oats in a bowl
  • Put hot water into the bowl
  • Add the toppings (bananas, Stawberries, Blueberries).
  • Add honey on top
  • Enjoy!
